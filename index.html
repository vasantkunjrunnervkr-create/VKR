About VKR

Vasant Kunj Runner (VKR) started as a small group of friends who shared a passion for fitness, running and healthy living.


What began as casual evening runs in Vasant Kunj has now grown into a community that welcomes runners from all backgrounds.


Our goal is simple: Run Together • Stay Fit • Build Friendships

Our Vision

To create an active running culture in Vasant Kunj and inspire people of all ages to embrace fitness through community running.


We aim to organize annual and monthly running events while building a strong and positive fitness community.
