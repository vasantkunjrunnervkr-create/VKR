WHO WE ARE

About VKR

Vasant Kunj Runners (VKR) started as a small group of friends who shared a passion for fitness, running and healthy living.

What began as casual morning runs in Vasant Kunj has now grown into a vibrant community that welcomes runners of all ages and fitness levels.

Our goal is simple — Run Together • Stay Fit • Build Friendships.

🏃 Community Running 💪 All Fitness Levels 🤝 Vasant Kunj 🌿 Nature Runs
🏆

Our Vision

To build an active running culture in Vasant Kunj and inspire people of all ages to embrace fitness through community running.


We plan to organize at least one community run every 3 months, bringing runners together regularly throughout the year.
