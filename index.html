WHO WE ARE

About VKR

Vasant Kunj Runners (VKR) is a running group based in Vasant Kunj, New Delhi. We bring together people who enjoy running, staying active, and supporting each other on their fitness journey.

Our goal is simple — to organize well-managed running events where runners of all experience levels feel welcome. Whether you're just starting or have been running for years, VKR is a place to participate, connect, and enjoy the experience.

Along with our running events, we also organize fun post-run activities and offer official VKR merchandise for our members.

At VKR, every run is about fitness, consistency, and enjoying the journey together.

🏃 Community Running 💪 All Fitness Levels 🤝 Vasant Kunj 🌿 Nature Runs

Our Vision

To inspire people to stay active through running and community fitness.


Our goal is to organize 2–3 community running events each year, creating opportunities for runners to connect, stay motivated, and enjoy running together.
