About VKR

Vasant Kunj Runner (VKR) started as a small group of friends who shared a passion for running, fitness and healthy living.


What began as casual evening runs in Vasant Kunj has grown into a community that promotes fitness, friendship and community participation.


VKR welcomes runners of all levels and aims to create memorable experiences through community runs and marathon events.

Our Vision

To build a welcoming running community where anyone can join, participate in community runs and be part of annual marathon events.


Our goal is to grow from a group of friends into a platform that inspires people to stay active, stay healthy and connect through fitness and friendship.

Events

Annual Community Run


VKR currently organizes annual running events and community gatherings.


Future plans include organizing regular monthly runs and expanding participation across Delhi and beyond.
